Managing Your Finances

Personal finance is all about managing your income, expenses, and investments to achieve financial independence. Here are some essential tips:

  • Create and stick to a budget.
  • Build an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of expenses.
  • Invest early and consistently for long-term goals.
  • Minimize debt and use credit wisely.
